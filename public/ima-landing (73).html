Questions

Common Questions

Do I need specialized instructors? +
The on-screen instructor leads the workout, so your staff only needs minimal martial arts experience. You'll need one person per class to supervise, keep energy high, and manage students — the TV does the heavy lifting.
How much space do I need? +
A 4-row setup requires a minimum 30' x 30' floor space — which most existing martial arts studios already have. 8 rows requires 60' x 30', and 12 rows needs 90' x 30'.
How quickly can I launch? +
Most studios are up and running within 1-2 weeks of signing up. Setup involves mounting TVs, plugging in the pre-configured devices, and a 30-minute onboarding call with our team.
What age group is this designed for? +
The program is designed for kids ages 6-14, which is the core homeschool demographic. The three levels accommodate different ages and ability levels within that range.
Can I charge more than $243/month per student? +
Absolutely. The $243 figure is a conservative baseline. Many studios charge $275-$350/month for specialized programs like homeschool athletic training. The revenue projections only go up from there.
Is this exclusive to my market? +
We don't currently offer territory exclusivity, but we do limit the number of studios we onboard per market to maintain program quality. Book a call to find out availability in your area.
